Mylo's Butter Chicken Curry

By Mylo


  • Step One: Marinate your cubed chicken breast in a bowl for 30 minutes
  • Step Two: Cook cubed chicken breast in a pot with half a stick of butter. While doing this, start cooking rice and garlic naan
  • Step Three: Once chicken is browned, remove from pot. Add another quarter of butter to the pot. Once butter is dissolved, add heavy whipping cream, tomato sauce, cinnamon stick, and some additional chili powder (chili powder optional if you like the spice)
  • Step Four: Add the chicken back to the pot and let it simmer for 15 minutes on medium-low heat
  • Step Five: Serve with rice and garlic naan. Enjoy!
