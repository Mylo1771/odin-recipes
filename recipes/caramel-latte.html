Mylo's Special Caramel Latte

By Mylo


  • Step One: Grab your finest cup and pump 3 shots of salted caramel syrup into it
  • Step Two: Using the same cup, pull a 16g double-shot of espresso into it
  • Step Three: Using a milk frothing pitcher, froth your milk until it reaches preferred temperature (will require knowledge on how to froth milk properly)
  • Step Four: Star pouring from up high and slowly bring the spout to the base of your milk to make your desired latte-art shape.
  • Step Five: Enjoy!
