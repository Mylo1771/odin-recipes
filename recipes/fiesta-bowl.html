Noel's Special Fiesta Bowl

By Mylo


  • Step One: Start cooking your rice. Once the rice cooker is working it's magic, start grilling up some cubed up and marinated chicken breast
  • Step Two: As the chicken is cooking, chop up some onions, green bell peppers, and sautee them with some olive oil on the side
  • Step Three: Once everything is cooked and ready to go, go ahead and divy up the rice, chicken, and your sauted veggies
  • Step Four: Add your pepita seeds, feta cheese, chipotle sauce, and mix it all together nice and good
  • Step Five: Enjoy!
